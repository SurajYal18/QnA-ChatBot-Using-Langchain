# Buzz Organizations

## Buzz Organizations Structure

Description: This section details the hierarchical structure within a Buzz Organization, outlining the roles and responsibilities of each user type.

*   **Super Administrator (Admin):**  The highest-level administrator with complete control over the organization.
*   **Buzz Org Administrator (Admin):**  Administrators with significant control over organizational settings and members.
*   **Buzz Org Manager(s):**  Managers who can manage members and features within the organization.  They cannot remove Admins or other Managers.
*   **Buzz Org Members:** Regular members of the organization.
*   **Buzz Org Associates:** Buzz users within an organization with limited privileges.


## Buzz Organizational Features

Description: This section describes the key features available within Buzz Organizations, focusing on member management and data security.

*   **Admin Role:** Admins can grant other members Manager privileges.
*   **Manager Role:** Managers can promote other members to Manager roles, but cannot remove Admins or other Managers.
*   **Group Management:** Groups can be linked to an organization. Only members of the organization can join these groups, and members lose access to group messages upon leaving the organization.
*   **Message Visibility:** Messages exchanged between members are visible only while they remain part of the organization.
*   **Org Fax Management:** Organizations can manage their fax number by associating it with a member's BuzzFax.


## Super Admin

Description: The Super Admin is a foundational role in Buzz, ensuring administrative continuity, compliance, and control within the organization.  It is automatically created upon organization setup.

**Step-by-Step Guide (Not Provided)**

*   **Continuity & Oversight:** Acts as a backup for Org Admins in case of absence or departure, automatically participates in all authorized conversations for traceability, enforces group policies, and can reassign group admins or managers.



## Organization Admin & Manager

Description: This section explains the roles and permissions of Organization Admins and Managers.

*   **Admin:** There is one Admin per organization, responsible for making decisions regarding all users and operational settings.  The Admin can assign any Org member as a Manager.
*   **Manager:** There can be multiple Managers.  Their functionality is similar to that of an Admin, enabling them to assist with operations.  Managers cannot change the privileges of Admins or other Managers.


## Associate Buzz User

Description: Associate users have limited privileges within a Buzz Organization.

**List of features that are NOT ALLOWED:**

*   Create groups
*   Find and add users to their Buzz Circle
*   See other Organizational Users
*   Reply Privately to a user from a Group Message
*   Forward Message to other users
*   Invite users to join Buzz or send BuzzLink & BuzzVideo invites
*   Initiate Buzz audio or video calls

If a Buzz user initiates a 1:1 conversation with an Associate user, a group conversation is automatically created to prevent direct 1:1 communication from the Associate user back to the organizational Buzz user.
